Step-by-Step Guide

Complete Instagram Download Guide

Follow this detailed guide to download Instagram content on any device. Works for Reels, videos, photos, stories and IGTV.

1

Find the Instagram Content You Want to Download

Open Instagram on your mobile device or visit instagram.com on your desktop browser. Browse your feed, search for a specific creator, or navigate directly to a profile. Find the specific Reel, video, post or story you want to save. Make sure the account is set to public — InstaSave can only access content from public profiles.

2

Copy the Direct Link to the Post

On iPhone/iPad: Tap the three-dot (…) icon on the post, then tap Copy Link. On Android: Tap the share icon, then select Copy Link. On desktop: Click the three-dot menu on the post and choose Copy Link. The Instagram URL is now in your clipboard. It will look something like instagram.com/reel/ABC123xyz or instagram.com/p/ABC123xyz.

3

Paste the URL into InstaSave

Come back to InstaSave and tap or click the input field at the top of the page. Tap the Paste URL button, or press Ctrl+V on Windows/Linux, or Cmd+V on Mac. On mobile, long-press the input field and select Paste. The Instagram link will appear in the field — double-check it looks correct before proceeding.

4

Click Download and Wait for Processing

Press the blue Download button. InstaSave's servers will process your request — this typically takes 1–3 seconds. If the first attempt doesn't succeed, our system automatically retries using alternate servers. You'll see a loading indicator during processing. Do not refresh the page while your download is being prepared.

5

Preview and Save the File to Your Device

Once ready, a media preview card appears showing your content's thumbnail and quality details. Click Download Video to save the file. On desktop, it saves to your Downloads folder in MP4 or JPEG format. On mobile, it saves to your Photos app or Downloads folder. The download is complete — no watermarks, no quality reduction, exactly as originally posted.
